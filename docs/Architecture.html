Key findings
Loop 1 zero standalone benefit — 160 runs, zero exceptions
Trust reweighting without planning adaptation is architecturally insufficient. Loop 1 and Loop 2 are a coupled mechanism — neither works without the other.
Combined loops required at extreme degradation (HAZ-08)
Loop 2 alone fails at glare=0.90 + LiDAR=0.80. Combined config: EMERGENCY triggered, collision prevented. SG3 + SG5 verified.
FPC ≤ 1.0 across all measured scenarios — no amplification
Loop 2 never amplifies injected failures (FPC never exceeds 1.0). Degree of attenuation is graduated by scenario: complete (FPC=0.0) in rain/night/construction; mild (FPC 0.87–0.92) in fog/occlusion cases; near-full transmission (FPC=0.99) in the most extreme combined-failure scenario, where Combined config rather than Loop 2 alone is required.
